About Me#

I am a French engineer who started a PhD in neuroscience. I work at the Functional Brain Mapping Lab where I study brain wave (EEG). As a researcher, I am particularly interested in EEG microstates for both their theoretical approach and their clinical application and currently develop a framework for using these biomarkers as targets for neurofeedback therapies, with a special focus on attention deficit and hyperactivity disorder.

As a researcher engineer, I try to play my part by making analysis tools and techniques accessible to the largest number of people. I have the deep conviction that this is the key element that will advance our knowledge about the brain. That is why I actively participate in the development of the open source python ecosystem: I contribute to libraries such as nipype and MNE, and complement them with libraries I wrote such as Pycartool and Pycrostates.
